PlayMode

Plays an animation that is listed in the creature's puppet. Syntax:

animID=PlayMode(thing, submode);

thing: This is the thing whose puppet is used and the thing that the animation will be played with.

submode: This is the number of a submode in a puppet file. Puppet keys are not listed in order of their submode number, but a comment beside each key in ky.pup lists its submode number.
