AISetInterest

Sets the thing of interest for an actor. Syntax:

AISetInterest(actor, interest);

If the thing of interest is a player, the actor's interest will instead be set to -1. Otherwise, if the actor is not already attacking another target, he will attack the thing of interest. If the actor's interest dies or is destroyed, AIGetInterest continues to return the same interest thing number until another call to AISetInterest is made.
