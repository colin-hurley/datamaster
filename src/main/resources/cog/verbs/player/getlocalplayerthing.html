GetLocalPlayerThing

Returns the thing number of the local player. Syntax:

player=GetLocalPlayerThing();

It's been said that GetLocalPlayerThing(), jkGetLocalPlayer(), or both have problems in MP with returning the correct player - these verbs allegedly returned only the host player's thing number. Testing does not confirm this, and the problem with these verbs is probably the fault of users not understanding how their cogs were acting in MP.
