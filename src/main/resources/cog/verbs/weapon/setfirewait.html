SetFireWait

Players have to wait for the firewait delay to end before they can fire or mount weapons. SetFireWait() resets this delay. If called while a weapon is firing, SetFireWait() also resets the firewait delay originally set by ActivateWeapon(), which will change how long the engine waits before running the fire message again. Syntax:

SetFireWait(player, firewait);

Resetting the firewait delay will delay certain weapon-related messages from running until the firewait delay has elapsed, including activated, fire, selected, and deselected.
