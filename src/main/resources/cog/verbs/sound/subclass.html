Subclass Listing

The following table contains the actions and their numbers that are used in .snd files. The numbers are obtained from the action's listing in the exe. EG, create has a subclass number of 1 because it is the second sound action in the exe's list.

The actual submode numbers of actions in a puppet file differ greatly from their number in the exe. This may be the case with these subclass numbers. They have not been tested.
