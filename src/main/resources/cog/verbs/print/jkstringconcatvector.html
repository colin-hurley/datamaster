jkStringConcatVector

Concatenates a vector. As with PrintVector(), the vector will be concatenated in this form: <x.000000 y.000000 z.000000>. Syntax:

jkStringConcatVector(vector);

vector: Remember that Cog treats all three-valued variables as vectors. You may concat positions, RGB values, velocities, or any value in vector form with this verb. You can also give the vector in 'x y z' form.
