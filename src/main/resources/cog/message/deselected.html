Deselected

1) Deselected is sent by a weapon's bin when a player selects a different weapon.

SenderAlthough a senderref is mentioned in weap_bryar, it is not used.
SourceThe player who selected a new weapon.
Sender TypeSystem (1).
Source TypeThing (3).

2) Deselected is also sent by a force power's bin when it is deselected in the force power selection box.

SenderThe bin of the power that was deselected.
SourceThe player who deselected the power.
Sender Type1 if the player selects the power to the right, or 0 if he selects to the left.
Source TypeThing (3).
