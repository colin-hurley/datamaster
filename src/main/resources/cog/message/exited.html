Exited

1) Exited is sent by a sector when a thing exits it.

SenderThe sector that the thing left.
SourceThe thing that left the sector.
Sender TypeSector (5).
Source TypeThing (3).

2) Exited is also sent by a thing or surface when something is detached from it.

SenderThe thing or surface that something was detached from.
SourceThe thing that was detached.
Sender TypeThing (3) or Surface (6).
Source TypeThing (3).
