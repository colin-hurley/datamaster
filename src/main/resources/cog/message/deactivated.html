Deactivated

Deactivated is sent by an item, weapon, or force power's bin when its assigned hotkey is released. Deactivated will not run when the the key of a plain hotkey is released.

SenderThe bin of the item or force power that was deactivated.1
SourceThe player that released the hotkey.
Sender TypeSystem (1).
Source TypeThing (3).

1For weapon cogs, the senderref is the mode of fire: 0 for primary, 1 for secondary.
