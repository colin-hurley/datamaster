Exitbubble

Exitbubble is sent by a player when he exits another thing's bubble created by jkCreateBubble. The message is sent to all of the player's linked cogs (including class, capture, and level-linked cogs), as well as all inventory cogs that are both available and have the 0x8 inventory flag set.

SenderThe player who exited the bubble.
SourceNone.
Sender TypeThing (3).
Source TypeNone.
Param0The bubble type.
Param1None.
Param2None.
Param3None.
