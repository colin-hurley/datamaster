Enterbubble

Enterbubble is sent by a player when he enters another thing's bubble created by jkCreateBubble. The message is sent to all of the player's linked cogs (including class, capture, and level-linked cogs), as well as all inventory cogs that are both available and have the 0x8 inventory flag set. Only players cause this message to be sent, while other thing types, including actors, do not.

Enterbubble will be sent under any circumstance that causes a player to enter a bubble region. This could result from movement of the player or a bubble thing, or if a bubble is resized or created.
