Debug Mode Flags
HexPurpose
0x1Completely disables AI.
0x2Pauses keyframe animations on their first frame.
0x4Unknown.
0x8Unknown.
0x10Unknown.
0x20Unknown.
0x40A string is printed when a weapon explodes stating the surface, sector, and material of the location.
0x80Actors are aggressive, but do not damage the player. Dark Jedi are exceptions.
0x100Objects in the level retain their positions and status when the player dies and the level restarts.
0x200Actors are non-aggressive and run from players.
0x400Slow motion gameplay.
