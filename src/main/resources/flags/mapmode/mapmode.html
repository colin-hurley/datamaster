Map Mode Flags
HexDecWhile set:
0x11Unknown.
0x22Entire map is visible, but not sectors hidden from the map.
0x44Players are visible.
0x88Actors are visible
0x1016Powerups are visible.1
0x2032Weapons and Debris are visible.1
0x4064All things are visible.
1These flags must be set with 0x4 or 0x8 or they do nothing.
