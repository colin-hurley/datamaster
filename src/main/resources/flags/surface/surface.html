Surface Flags
HexPurpose
0x1Surface is a floor.
0x2Surface will send messages to its associated cogs.
0x4Surface is impassible.
0x8Surface floor is not walkable by AI actors.
0x10Surface texture scrolls at double velocity.
0x20Surface texture scrolls at half velocity.
0x40Surface texture scrolls at 1/8 velocity.
0x80Surface will not cause impact damage.
0x100Unknown.
0x200Surface will be rendered as a horizon sky.
0x400Surface will be rendered as a ceiling sky.
0x800Surface is scrolling.
0x1000Surface gives increased momentum to things attached to it.
0x2000Same as 0x1000, except things are given even more momentum.
0x4000Surface is magnetically sealed.
0x8000Unknown.
0x10000Surface floor is metal - causes metal walksounds.
0x20000Surface is a water floor.
0x40000Surface is a puddle floor.
0x80000Surface floor is dirt - causes dirt walksounds.
0x100000Surface is a floor of deeper water.
