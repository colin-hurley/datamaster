0x8000 Weapon Flag

The 0x8000 flag is a dependent flag of 0x2000 or 0x10000. When either combination is used, the weapon will use its elementsize and range template settings to create a string of trailthings from the projectile's starting point to its projected destination.

The 0x10000 gives the added effect of semi-random direction for each trailthing; the weapon will use its trailcylradius and trailrandangle to make the trailthings only loosely follow the path of the parent weapon. More at 0x10000.
