Weapon Flags

Weapon Flags are the typeflags for things of type weapon. They are initially set in the template, but they can be modified by Cog after they are created. All of the known Weapon Flags act as pregame settings.

It is unfortunate that so many Weapon Flags remain unknown to us, because things of the weapon type offer great potential to JK patch authors. Multiplayer testing was done for all of these unknown flags, but still no purpose could be found.
